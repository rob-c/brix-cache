4/*
5 * rrdata.h — typed decode of a CMS request/forwarded-op payload (XrdOucPup).
6 *
7 * WHAT: mirrors the subset of XrdCms's XrdCmsRRData that nginx-xrootd needs to
8 * route and execute forwarded namespace ops and request frames. One
9 * parser, keyed by opcode, fills an brix_cms_rrdata_t whose string spans
10 * borrow the caller's payload buffer.
11 * WHY: byte-exact interop with stock cmsd. The on-wire field order per opcode is
12 * fixed by XrdCmsParser's fwdArgA/B/C, padArgs, pdlArgs and locArgs Pup arg
13 * vectors; this decoder reproduces them exactly.
14 * HOW: pure C (no nginx headers) so it is unit-testable standalone, matching the
15 * ini.c / zip_dir.c pattern. Pup "char" fields are length-prefixed strings
16 * ([2B BE len incl. NUL][bytes][NUL]); "int" fields are tag(0xa0)+4B BE.
17 * The decoded string length EXCLUDES the trailing wire NUL, and because the
18 * NUL is present in the buffer the spans are usable as C strings in place.
19 */
20
