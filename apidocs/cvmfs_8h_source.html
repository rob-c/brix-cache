1/* cvmfs.h — the cvmfs:// protocol surface.
2 *
3 * WHAT: loc-conf + request-ctx types, the handler entry, and the gate/geo
4 * prototypes for the dedicated CVMFS protocol plane.
5 * WHY: cvmfs:// is a first-class protocol (peer of webdav/, s3/): its own
6 * module owns configuration and its own content handler owns every
7 * request — WebDAV dispatch is never involved.
8 * HOW: the loc-conf embeds the SAME shared preamble (`common`) the other
9 * HTTP protocols embed, so brix_cvmfs_storage_backend /
10 * brix_cvmfs_cache_store compose the identical phase-63/64 storage
11 * stack underneath a protocol-specific top.
12 */
