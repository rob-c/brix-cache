4/*
5 * pelican_register.h — advertise THIS node as a discoverable Pelican cache.
6 *
7 * WHAT: Periodically POST a signed OriginAdvertiseV2 document to the federation
8 * Director's /api/v1.0/director/registerCache endpoint so the Director
9 * redirects clients to this cache. The POST carries a short-lived ES256
10 * advertise JWT (scope "pelican.advertise") signed with the cache's key.
11 * WHY: Phase 3a let the module *pull* from a Pelican federation; this is the
12 * complement — joining the federation as a published cache. Pelican
13 * caches/origins re-advertise on a ~1-minute cadence; the Director expires
14 * ads that stop arriving.
15 * HOW: A per-worker timer (armed in init_process when brix_cache_advertise is
16 * on) offloads each advertisement to the cache thread pool: discover the
17 * Director (.well-known/pelican-configuration), build the advertise JSON
18 * (jansson) and the ES256 JWT (jwt_sign.c), then libcurl POST it. The
19 * cache's public key must already be registered with the federation
20 * registry (an out-of-band operator step — the registry handshake is not
21 * performed here).
22 *
23 * Exact wire details (PelicanPlatform/pelican server_structs/director.go,
24 * origin/advertise.go) are documented inline in pelican_register.c.
25 */
