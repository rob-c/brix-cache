1/*
2 * stream_mirror.h — Phase 24 XRootD stream traffic mirror.
3 *
4 * Off by default. When `brix_stream_mirror_url host:port` is configured on a
5 * server block, a qualifying READ request (kXR_stat/locate/open/dirlist/statx,
6 * per brix_mirror_opcodes) is replayed fire-and-forget to one or more shadow
7 * XRootD servers AFTER the primary handler has already answered the client.
8 * The shadow connection runs the same bootstrap as the health-check probe
9 * (handshake -> protocol -> login), then sends the saved request frame, reads
10 * the response status, discards the body, and compares the status against the
11 * primary to detect divergence. The client is never delayed and never sees the
12 * shadow response.
13 *
14 * Memory: the mirror context is allocated from ngx_cycle->pool (not the client
15 * connection pool) because the client connection may close before the shadow
16 * exchange completes — see Phase 24 Step D.
17 */
