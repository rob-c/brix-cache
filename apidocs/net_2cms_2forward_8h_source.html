4/*
5 * forward.h — manager-side Plane B fan-out primitive.
6 *
7 * WHAT: encode a forwarded namespace op (chmod/mkdir/mkpath/mv/rm/rmdir/trunc)
8 * and transmit it to one data-node CMS connection, byte-exact with stock
9 * cmsd's forwarded request frames.
10 * WHY: the reusable core of manager fan-out: the manager calls this once per
11 * eligible node when a client mutates the namespace. The node executes it
12 * confined (see node_ops.c) and replies silent-on-success / kYR_error.
13 * HOW: brix_cms_rrdata_encode() builds the Pup payload; brix_cms_send_frame()
14 * writes the header+payload on the given connection. The caller chooses the
15 * streamid (its correlation key for aggregating replies) and the nodes.
16 *
17 * NOTE: the client trigger (manager-mode root:// mutation handler), eligible-node
18 * selection across workers, and reply aggregation are the remaining
19 * manager-side integration — see the Plane B design spec. This primitive is
20 * the byte-exact, unit-tested wire half they build on.
21 */
