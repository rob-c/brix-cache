1/* brixautofs.h — pure core of the CVMFS-brix automount umbrella (no libfuse).
2 *
3 * WHAT: the FQRN validator, the repo-slot table state machine, and the
4 * CVMFS_REPOSITORIES membership test used by the `brixMount autofs`
5 * umbrella driver (brixautofs.c).
6 * WHY: the umbrella is the security boundary for attacker-chosen lookup
7 * names on /cvmfs — the pure parts live here so the unit test builds
8 * them without libfuse (compile brixautofs.c with -DBRIXAUTOFS_UNIT).
9 */
