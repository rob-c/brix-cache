1/*
2 * reqid_map.h — CMS↔engine request-id sidecar map (Phase-89 W2 / ADR-2b).
3 *
4 * WHAT: A small SHM slot table keyed by the MANAGER's prepadd reqid (the id a
5 * cmsd manager echoes in kYR_prepdel), holding the ENGINE reqid the
6 * stage-request registry minted for that admission plus the two padArgs
7 * fields the registry's live view has no columns for: notify and prty.
8 *
9 * WHY: The stage-request registry is the durable truth for a request's
10 * lifecycle, but it keys by its own "<seq>.<pid>@<host>" reqid — a
11 * forwarded kYR_prepdel only carries the manager's id. ADR-2b (phase-89
12 * App C.0.1) keeps the registry view untouched and carries the mapping
13 * (and notify/prty) in this sidecar instead of widening the durable
14 * record. SHM, not per-worker: the prepdel may arrive on a different
15 * worker's manager connection than the prepadd did after a reconnect.
16 *
17 * HOW: fnv1a open-addressing over BRIX_CMS_REQID_MAP_SLOTS (power of two),
18 * last-writer-wins per cms reqid, entries lazily expired after
19 * BRIX_CMS_REQID_MAP_TTL_MS (the engine reap horizon — a mapping older
20 * than that points at a request the registry reaper has already
21 * retired). Table created via the brix_shm_table_* helpers
22 * (INVARIANT #10, never a bare ngx_shmtx_create); zone registered by
23 * brix_cms_reqid_map_configure() appended LAST in
24 * postconf_shared_registries (zone order is the reload contract).
25 */
