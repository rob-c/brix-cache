1/* cvmfs_module_internal.h — cross-file seam for the split cvmfs:// module glue.
2 *
3 * WHAT: Declares the handful of former-static entry points that the cvmfs
4 * module's directive table, config-merge orchestrator, and export-build
5 * step call across the translation units they were split into
6 * (module.c ↔ cvmfs_module_georank.c ↔ cvmfs_module_merge.c ↔
7 * cvmfs_module_build.c).
8 * WHY: module.c grew past the file-size gate, so its directive-parse callbacks,
9 * geographic origin ranking, config-field merge, and enabled-branch
10 * storage build were lifted into sibling files. Every symbol that is
11 * DEFINED in one of those files but CALLED from another must be a
12 * non-static, header-declared prototype — this header is that single
13 * shared declaration point. Nothing here is part of the public cvmfs://
14 * surface (that lives in cvmfs.h); these are module-internal seams only.
15 * HOW: Include after cvmfs.h (it supplies ngx_http_brix_cvmfs_loc_conf_t and
16 * the nginx config types). Each declaration names its defining file so a
17 * reader can jump straight to the implementation.
18 */
