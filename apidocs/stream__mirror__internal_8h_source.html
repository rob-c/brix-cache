1/*
2 * stream_mirror_internal.h — declarations shared across the XRootD stream
3 * traffic-mirror files after the phase-79 file-size split.
4 *
5 * WHAT: Cross-declares the shadow-connection context struct, its phase enum, the
6 * one metric-increment macro, the replay payload bound, and the single
7 * engine entry point (brix_mir_start) that straddle the
8 * stream_mirror.c / stream_mirror_launch.c boundary.
9 * WHY: stream_mirror.c (844 lines) split into three focused files under the
10 * 500-line cap: stream_mirror.c (the async shadow-connection state machine
11 * — bootstrap/replay/read/dispatch/lifecycle), stream_mirror_launch.c
12 * (opcode filter + eligibility gates + per-target launch + the public
13 * brix_stream_mirror_maybe hook), and stream_mirror_config.c (directive
14 * setters). The launch driver allocates a brix_stream_mirror_t, snapshots
15 * the request into it, and calls brix_mir_start() — exactly the struct, the
16 * phase enum, the metric macro, the payload bound, and brix_mir_start()
17 * cross that boundary and are declared here; nothing else crosses. The
18 * config setters are self-contained and do not include this header.
19 * HOW: stream_mirror.c and stream_mirror_launch.c both include this header;
20 * none of these symbols is part of the mirror's public surface
21 * (stream_mirror.h). brix_mir_start is the ONLY symbol here with external
22 * linkage — it is defined non-static in stream_mirror.c.
23 *
24 * Requires: stream_mirror.h (→ core/ngx_brix_module.h, which transitively
25 * provides XRD_RESPONSE_HDR_LEN and the brix metrics accessor used by
26 * BRIX_MIR_METRIC_INC) included before this header, plus <sys/socket.h>
27 * for struct sockaddr_storage / socklen_t.
28 */
