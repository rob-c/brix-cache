4/*
5 * relay_guard.h — bad-actor guard sink for the transparent stream relay.
6 *
7 * WHAT: per-relay guard state plus the tap sink that classifies each decoded
8 * root:// frame (requests pre-verdict, responses post-signal) and asks the
9 * relay to drop the connection on a BOUNCE.
10 * WHY: the relay is a credential-preserving MITM in front of a real XRootD
11 * server; the guard gives it the same junk-bounce + fail2ban audit contract
12 * the HTTP guard module gives ARC/XrdHttp (see src/net/guard/).
13 * HOW: registered as an additional sink on the relay's tap; enforcement is a
14 * drop flag the relay pump checks after every tap feed (a stream has no
15 * "403" — the bounce is connection teardown).
16 */
