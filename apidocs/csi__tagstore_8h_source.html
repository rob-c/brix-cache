10/*
11 * fs/backend/csi_tagstore.h — block-granule checksum integrity on the unified
12 * metadata record (xmeta P3; replaces the phase-59 per-page ".xrdt" sidecar).
13 *
14 * WHAT: One CRC32C per cinfo block (the record's buffer_size granule, default
15 * brix_csi_block = 1MiB), stored in the file's own xmeta record
16 * (BLOCKCRC section) — user.xrd.cinfo xattr or "<path>.cinfo" sidecar,
17 * via the shared fs/meta/xmeta_path.c carrier. No more tag tree.
18 *
19 * WHY: One form of metadata per file (spec 2026-07-02-xmeta-unified-
20 * metadata-design.md §CSI): integrity tags live beside the cache /
21 * write-back state in a single record readable by stock cinfo tools.
22 *
23 * HOW: Reads verify only the blocks they FULLY span against the recorded
24 * CRCs (a slot of 0 = "not computed" and is skipped; partial-edge
25 * blocks are covered by verify-on-fill and scrub, not the hot path).
26 * Writes never touch the record: the engine folds CRCs of fully
27 * covered blocks into a handle-local table as the bytes stream through
28 * and merges them into the record ONCE at close (brix_csi_flush),
29 * recomputing up to BRIX_CSI_FLUSH_READ blocks of unaligned edges
30 * from disk. Fail-closed: a crash before flush leaves stale CRCs, so
31 * reads of a torn upload fail kXR_ChkSumErr until rewritten.
32 */
33
