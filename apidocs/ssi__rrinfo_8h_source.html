4/*
5 * ssi_rrinfo.h — byte-exact codec for the XrdSsi request/response control words.
6 *
7 * WHAT: pack/unpack the 8-byte XrdSsiRRInfo carried in the kXR read/write/truncate
8 * offset field, and build the 16-byte XrdSsiRRInfoAttn prefix that heads a
9 * kXR_attn response/alert push.
10 * WHY: byte-exact interop with a real libXrdSsi client. The layouts are fixed by
11 * XrdSsi/XrdSsiRRInfo.hh; this codec is validated against golden values
12 * generated from that very class (see ssi_rrinfo_unittest.c).
13 * HOW: pure C (no nginx headers) so it is unit-testable standalone. The RRInfo
14 * offset, serialized big-endian as the kXR offset, lays out as
15 * [id_lo][id_mid][id_hi][cmd][size little-endian u32]; RRInfoAttn is
16 * [tag][flags][pfxLen BE u16][mdLen BE u32][8 reserved bytes].
17 */
