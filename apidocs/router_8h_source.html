4/*
5 * router.h — table-driven CMS opcode routing, mirroring XrdCmsRouting.cc.
6 *
7 * WHAT: per-role descriptor tables mapping a kYR_* opcode to its name and wire
8 * routing flags. Both CMS halves dispatch through brix_cms_route_lookup:
9 * the manager (frames accepted up from data nodes + forwardable client ops)
10 * and the data node (forwarded ops accepted down from its manager).
11 * WHY: byte-exact parity with stock cmsd's routing — the FORWARD/REPLIABLE/
12 * DELAYABLE/SYNC/NOARGS flags drive whether the manager fans an op out to
13 * nodes and how replies are aggregated, exactly as initRDRrouting /
14 * initRouter / initMANrouting prescribe.
15 * HOW: pure C (no nginx headers) so the table is unit-testable standalone.
16 */

18/* Wire routing flags (subset of XrdCmsRouting bits relevant to dispatch). */
