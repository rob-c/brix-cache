1/*
2 * Copyright (C) 2025-2026 HEP-x Contributors
3 *
4 * tpc_cred.h — HTTP-TPC credential delegation declarations
5 *
6 * Provides OAuth2/OIDC access-token acquisition for third-party copy
7 * pull transfers. Two delegation modes are supported:
8 *
9 * 1. oidc-agent — UNIX-socket JSON IPC to a local oidc-agent daemon
10 * 2. token-exchange — RFC 8693 token-exchange request to an external
11 * OAuth2 token endpoint
12 *
13 * The caller (tpc.c) invokes webdav_tpc_cred_obtain_token() after parsing
14 * the Credential: header from the COPY request. On success the returned
15 * ngx_str_t contains a raw Bearer token that the caller injects into the
16 * transfer_headers array before launching the curl subprocess.
17 */
18
