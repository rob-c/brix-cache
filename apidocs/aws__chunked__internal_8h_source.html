1/*
2 * aws_chunked_internal.h — declarations shared across the aws-chunked
3 * request-body decoder files after the phase-79 file-size split.
4 *
5 * WHAT: Cross-declares the framing-parser context (s3_chunk_ctx_t), its parser
6 * state enum (s3_chunk_state_t), the two line/extension caps, and the
7 * exactly-two functions that call across the decoder's translation units:
8 * s3_chunk_fail() (the state-machine failure primitive) and s3_chunk_feed()
9 * (feed one contiguous span through the machine). Everything else stays
10 * file-local (static).
11 * WHY: aws_chunked.c (801 lines) split into three focused files under the
12 * 500-line cap: aws_chunked.c (decode orchestrator: context setup,
13 * buffer-chain drain, public entry point), aws_chunked_parse.c (the
14 * security-relevant byte-level framing state machine + per-chunk SigV4
15 * signature verification), and aws_chunked_encoding.c (Content-Encoding /
16 * x-amz-content-sha256 detection and inner-coding classification). The
17 * orchestrator and the parser share one context struct and the two
18 * cross-called entry points below; nothing else crosses a file boundary.
19 * HOW: aws_chunked.c and aws_chunked_parse.c include this header; the two
20 * declared symbols are non-static and carry no linkage beyond the decoder
21 * translation units. aws_chunked_encoding.c needs none of this and does
22 * not include it.
23 *
24 * Requires: aws_chunked.h (s3_chunk_trailer_t, s3_chunk_verify_t, ngx request
25 * and integer types) is included here.
26 */
