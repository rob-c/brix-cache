1/*
2 * pwd.h — XRootD `pwd` (XrdSecpwd) password-auth constants + password-file API.
3 *
4 * WHAT: Shared declarations for the Phase 52 WS-B password protocol: the XrdSecpwd
5 * step codes and pwdStatus_t fields used on the wire, plus the password-file
6 * lookup/verify helpers (src/pwd/pwdfile.c).
7 * WHY: pwd is XRootD's legacy password scheme. It is opt-in, TLS-gated, and the
8 * password never touches disk in cleartext — brix_pwd_file stores only a
9 * PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA1 salted hash (the exact KDF stock XrdSecpwd uses).
10 * HOW: The handshake (src/pwd/auth.c) drives a 2-round DH-bootstrapped exchange;
11 * round 2 decrypts the client credential and verifies it via the helpers
12 * declared here against the configured brix_pwd_file.
13 *
14 * Wire reference: docs/refactor/phase-52-pwd-wire-spec.md and the source at
15 * /tmp/xrootd-src/src/XrdSecpwd/XrdSecProtocolpwd.{cc,hh}.
16 */
