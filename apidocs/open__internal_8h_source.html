5 * open_internal.h — helpers split out of the kXR_open handler (open_request.c)
6 * to keep that god-function decomposed and each file under the size cap. The
7 * two heaviest, self-contained phases moved to their own files:
8 * - open_tpc.c TPC (third-party-copy) context detection + destination/
