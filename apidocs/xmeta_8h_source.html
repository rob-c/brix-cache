7/*
8 * fs/meta/xmeta.h — unified per-file metadata record codec (xmeta P1).
9 *
10 * WHAT: One record per file replacing the .cinfo(XCI1)/.xrdt/.cks metadata
11 * zoo. The leading bytes are BYTE-IDENTICAL to a stock XrdPfc cinfo v4
12 * file (version + Store POD + crc + present bitmap + AStat[] + crc), so
13 * stock tools (xrdpfc_print) read the prefix as a normal cinfo. Our
14 * extra state rides in TLV sections appended AFTER the stock trailing
15 * crc — stock readers consume sequentially and never see them.
16 *
17 * WHY: One form of metadata on disk per file (spec
18 * docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-02-xmeta-unified-metadata-design.md):
19 * cache present-bitmap, dirty/write-back state, whole-file digests and
20 * block-granule CSI CRCs live together, carried in the user.xrd.cinfo
21 * xattr when they fit and as a stock-readable "<file>.cinfo" sidecar
22 * when they don't (see xmeta_carrier.h).
23 *
24 * HOW: Pure C, ngx-free, malloc-based (standalone-testable like
25 * csi_unittest.c). Stock PODs are emitted verbatim (native x86-64
26 * layout, the same portability contract as stock XrdPfc); extension
27 * sections are {type, len, payload, crc32c} TLVs — unknown types are
28 * skipped on decode (forward compat), a bad section crc rejects the
29 * record (torn write).
30 */
31
