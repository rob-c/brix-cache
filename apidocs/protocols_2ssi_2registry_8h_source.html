4/*
5 * registry.h — per-worker SSI session registry (async-delivery guard).
6 *
7 * WHAT: maps a stable connection id → live session, validated by generation.
8 * WHY: an async completion (timer / thread-pool job) holds only {conn_id,
9 * generation, reqId} — never a raw connection pointer — so it can safely
10 * resolve the live session or be dropped if the connection has closed or
11 * the slot was recycled. This is the single use-after-free invariant for
12 * server-pushed SSI responses.
13 * HOW: a fixed per-worker table. add() on open, remove() on teardown (BEFORE the
14 * connection pool is freed); find() rejects a moved generation. Sessions
15 * are connection-bound to one worker, so no shared memory is needed.
16 */
