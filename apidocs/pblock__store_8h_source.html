4/*
5 * pblock_store.h — the packed-block storage engine.
6 *
7 * How a pblock backend lays a blob out on disk (blob-id generation, object-dir
8 * and per-block path computation) and moves bytes into and out of the fixed-size
9 * block files that make up a blob (write/read/remove/copy). Split out of
10 * sd_pblock.c so the on-disk block format is reviewable on its own, independent
11 * of the brix_sd_driver_t vtable adapters (open/pread/pwrite/…) that call into
12 * it. Pure engine: every function takes the export state explicitly and holds
13 * no globals.
