4/*
5 * gridftp/ftp_dc_sec.h — GridFTP data-channel GSI security core for the
6 * event-driven (ev/) engine.
7 *
8 * WHAT: the cert/identity operations a GSI-protected (DCAU A / PROT P) data
9 * channel needs — present the delegated user credential on the data SSL, apply
10 * the data-channel TLS policy, and PKIX-verify the peer's proxy chain while
11 * pinning its end-entity DN to the control-channel identity.
12 *
13 * WHY: GridFTP's protected data channel is a straight TLS session on the data
14 * socket (client ClientHello, no globus token framing). The event engine drives
15 * ngx_ssl_handshake off the data connection's events; the cert/identity logic is
16 * factored out here — independent of how the handshake is driven — so it is
17 * written, reviewed, and fixed once as the data-channel security boundary.
18 *
19 * HOW: the caller creates the SSL (SSL_new / ngx_ssl_create_connection) and binds
20 * the fd, then hands this module a brix_ftp_dc_sec_t view of the session's GSI
21 * state. brix_ftp_dc_load_deleg() installs the delegated proxy + chain + key;
22 * brix_ftp_dc_apply_policy() sets the TLS 1.2 cap / abrupt-EOF tolerance / peer-
23 * cert requirement; brix_ftp_dc_gsi_check() runs post-handshake to verify the
24 * peer chain and pin its DN. None of them touch the fd or drive the handshake.
25 */
