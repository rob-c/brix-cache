4/*
5 * dig.h — XrdDig-style remote diagnostics (§3).
6 *
7 * WHAT: read-only, authorization-gated exposure of whitelisted server files
8 * (config, logs, …) over HTTP under "/.well-known/dig/<export>/<rel>".
9 * WHY: operators need to fetch a node's config/logs remotely for diagnosis,
10 * the capability XrdDig provides — without a shell on the box.
11 * HOW: every access is (a) default-off, (b) confined to a config-declared export
12 * directory by the kernel openat2(RESOLVE_BENEATH) primitive (so "../" and
13 * symlink escapes are impossible), (c) fail-closed authorized against a
14 * principal→export allow-file, and (d) read-only (GET/HEAD only). The
15 * declared export dirs are realpath'd at config time (the BENEATH anchors).
16 *
17 * The public entry point is brix_dig_handle(), called from the WebDAV dispatch
18 * when the request path is under the dig prefix.
19 */
