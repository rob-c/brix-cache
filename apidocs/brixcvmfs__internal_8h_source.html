1/*
2 * brixcvmfs_internal.h — seam between the read-only CVMFS-brix FUSE driver
3 * (brixcvmfs.c) and the writable-overlay driver (brixcvmfs_rw.c).
4 *
5 * WHAT: exposes the mounted-client accessors and the read-only FUSE ops so the
6 * cvmfs-rw ops table can delegate every lower-layer case to them, plus
7 * the rw hooks brixcvmfs.c calls when the overlay is enabled.
8 * WHY: the rw driver is union logic ONLY — trust chain, catalogs, fetch and
9 * the read-only op semantics stay in one place; the union layer composes
10 * "overlay first, ro-op fallback" across the TU boundary.
11 * HOW: brixcvmfs.c owns the process-global mount state; brixcvmfs_rw.c owns
12 * the process-global overlay state. Both are single-threaded (-s).
13 */
