1/*
2 * sd_posix_internal.h — implementation-private definitions shared by the
3 * sd_posix_*.c units of the POSIX storage driver.
4 *
5 * WHAT: Holds the driver-private instance-state struct and the cross-unit
6 * prototypes for the vtable slot functions that were split out of
7 * sd_posix.c (the worker-safe raw byte ops in sd_posix_io.c and the
8 * nginx-coupled namespace/dir/xattr/staged ops in sd_posix_ns.c), plus
9 * the shared sd_posix_fill_stat helper. The registration descriptor
10 * (brix_sd_posix_driver) in sd_posix.c references these symbols; keeping
11 * them non-static + declared here lets the descriptor stay in one file.
12 *
13 * WHY: A behaviour-preserving file-size split needs the moved clusters to see
14 * the same struct/helper the descriptor does, with zero change to what
15 * each function does.
16 *
17 * HOW: Include-guarded. The raw byte ops are always compiled (they build into
18 * the ngx-free shared libxrdproto too); the namespace/instance ops are
19 * module-only and their prototypes sit under !XRDPROTO_NO_NGX to mirror
20 * their definitions.
21 */
22
