1/*
2 * diag_metabench.c — `xrddiag metabench <url>`: a concurrent metadata storm with
3 * ops/sec + p50/p95/p99, proving the CLIENT performs (not just connects). The
4 * metadata counterpart of diag_bench.c's download timing.
5 *
6 * Reuses client/lib/metabench so the workload is byte-for-byte identical to the
7 * pblock reliability suite's other layers (one source of truth). Each worker
8 * holds one persistent GSI/anon session (per a->conn) and creates → chmods →
9 * stats → removes its own /w<id> subtree.
10 *
11 * Tunables (shared with the rest of xrddiag): -S/--streams = worker count,
12 * --count = ops per worker. Exit non-zero on any op failure or a p99 breach so
13 * it doubles as an operator health gate.
14 */
