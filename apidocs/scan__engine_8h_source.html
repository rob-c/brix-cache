1/*
2 * scan_engine.h — VFS-driven walk + per-object action for the storage scan.
3 *
4 * WHAT: brix_scan_run() walks a confined subtree (brix_vfs_walk), runs one
5 * mode action per regular file, and appends one NDJSON record per file to
6 * a growing heap buffer, accumulating run totals.
7 * WHY: this is the engine body the HTTP endpoint (scan_http.c) drives on a
8 * thread-pool worker; it depends on the VFS (and thus nginx types) but
9 * never on a protocol — a future root:// scan opcode can drive it too.
10 * HOW: mode action = brix_integrity_get_fd over the read-only fd the walk
11 * hands us (dump = cache-only, verify = stored-vs-computed, fill =
12 * compute+persist on miss); the fd/open/traversal stay inside the VFS.
13 */
