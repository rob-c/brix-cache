4/*
5 * recv_frame.h — the per-PDU framing phases of the root:// recv loop, split out
6 * of recv.c so that translation unit holds only the slim event-loop skeleton
7 * (the connection/ dir already splits helpers this way: chain_helpers,
8 * write_helpers, event_sched).
9 *
10 * Every phase helper runs INSIDE ngx_stream_brix_recv's `for (;;)` loop but
11 * cannot itself break/continue/return the caller's loop, so each returns a
12 * brix_recv_step_t telling the loop what to do — exactly the control-code idiom
13 * already used by brix_recv_pre_loop / brix_recv_handoff_state. The logic moved
14 * here is byte-for-byte the original; only the loop-exit statements were
15 * translated to step codes.
16 */
