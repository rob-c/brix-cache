6 * WHAT: Declares the RFC-3820 GSI OID / RSA-strength constants, the
7 * standalone-build compatibility shim + <core/compat/safe_size.h> include

9 * TU boundary after the phase-79 file-size split: pxr_make_pci_ext(), which is
10 * DEFINED in proxy_req.c (alongside its static DER helpers) and CALLED from
11 * proxy_req_sign.c when it adds the proxy's critical proxyCertInfo extension.
12 *
13 * WHY: proxy_req.c exceeded the ~500-line file-size guard, so it was carved into
14 * three cohesive units — request build (proxy_req.c), issuance/signing
15 * (proxy_req_sign.c), and delegated-chain assembly (proxy_req_assemble.c).
16 * The OID constants are referenced by both build and sign; the proxyCertInfo
17 * builder is shared by both; and the assemble unit needs the overflow-checked
18 * size arithmetic from safe_size.h. Collecting the shared macros, the
19 * standalone shim, and the one crossing symbol here keeps every definition in
20 * exactly one place while preserving the original single-file crypto byte for
21 * byte (identical OIDs, identical proxyCertInfo encoding, identical guards).

23 * HOW: Requires proxy_req.h (the brix_gsi_* blob/buf/err types + public API)
