130/*
131 * WHAT: one classified finding, by reference — the argument block of dx_record().
132 * WHY: dx_record used to take the five finding fields positionally (6 params,
133 * over the parameter gate); one descriptor keeps every callsite a single
134 * expression with the fields in dx_finding storage order.
135 * HOW: callsites pass a compound literal ordered exactly as dx_finding stores
136 * it: probe, verdict, kxr, cause, remedy. cause/remedy may be NULL
137 * (recorded as ""). dx_record copies everything, so temporaries are safe.

147/*
148 * WHAT: credential selection for one scoped diagnostic connection.
149 * WHY: dx_connect_as took the three selection knobs positionally (7 params);
150 * grouping them names each knob at the callsite.
151 * HOW: force_anon suppresses credentials entirely; token_override (NULL =
