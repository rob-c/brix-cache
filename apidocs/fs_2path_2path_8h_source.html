77/*
78 * brix_authdb_query_t — one authdb authorization query (the "who + what" of a
79 * single access decision), grouped so the check takes a single descriptor.
80 *
81 * WHAT: Bundles the five inputs that identify an authdb access decision — the
82 * candidate <rules>, the requesting <identity>, its <peer_ip> (for host rules),
83 * the <resolved_path> being accessed, and the <needed_privs> bitmask.
84 *
85 * WHY: These fields always travel together as an immutable query; passing them
86 * as one descriptor keeps the check's arity low and its call sites readable
87 * without changing any value or its meaning. The log sink is deliberately NOT
88 * a member — it is a side-effecting output, hoisted to its own parameter.
89 *
90 * HOW: Fill each field 1:1 from the former positional arguments (no defaults,
91 * no derivation) and pass &query to brix_check_authdb_identity(). Borrows every
92 * pointer for the duration of the synchronous call; owns nothing.
93 */
