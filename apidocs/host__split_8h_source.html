1/*
2 * host_split.h — parse "host[:port]" / "[ipv6][:port]" (the PARSE side).
3 *
4 * WHAT: split an authority into an unbracketed host + a port, IPv6-literal aware.
5 * WHY: the native client reimplements this bracketed-IPv6 host:port split in ~5
6 * places (url.c, xrddiag, xrdmapc, mpxstats); this is the one ngx-free leaf
7 * they can share. (host_format.h is the inverse — the EMIT side.)
8 * HOW: pure libc string scan over a caller buffer; no ngx, no allocation. The
9 * server's net_target parser is ngx_str_t/pool-coupled, so it can't share
10 * this as-is today (a future ptr+len core could back both).
11 */
