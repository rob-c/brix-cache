4/*
5 * cms/fanout.h — Phase-89 W8: manager-side rm/rmdir fan-out to all holders.
6 *
7 * WHAT: With brix_cms_fanout on, a client kXR_rm/kXR_rmdir arriving at a
8 * manager is forwarded (brix_cms_forward_to_node) to EVERY registered
9 * node whose exports cover the path, instead of redirecting the client
10 * to a single node — stock cmsd's "rm from all holders" semantics.
11 * WHY: with replicated paths (two nodes exporting "/"), the shipped redirect
12 * deletes exactly one replica and leaves the rest live — a later locate
13 * resurrects the "deleted" file. Fan-out removes every copy.
14 * HOW: the node executor is SILENT on success and answers kYR_error on
15 * failure, so success cannot be positively counted; aggregation is a
16 * deadline window (brix_cms_fanout_window): the client is parked
17 * (pending table + XRD_ST_WAITING_CMS), every kYR_error folds into the
18 * slot (first error's code+text win, arrival order), and the finalizer —
19 * the window timer, or early when every forwarded node has errored —
20 * answers kXR_ok when no error arrived, else kXR_error with the folded
21 * text.
22 *
23 * SCOPE (v1, deliberate): only rm/rmdir (single-path, no mode), and only when
24 * THIS worker owns CMS connections to every registry holder of the path —
25 * per-worker aggregation needs no cross-worker signalling (same per-worker
26 * design as the pending table / W3 node list). Anything else falls back to
27 * the shipped single-node redirect, which is always safe.
28 */
