4/*
5 * node_ops.h — data-node execution of namespace ops forwarded by the manager.
6 *
7 * WHAT: when a manager forwards kYR_chmod/mkdir/mkpath/mv/rm/rmdir/trunc down to
8 * this data node, node_ops decodes the request, runs it against local
9 * storage UNDER KERNEL CONFINEMENT (openat2 RESOLVE_BENEATH), and — exactly
10 * like stock cmsd — stays silent on success or returns a kYR_error on
11 * failure (ecode always kYR_EINVAL, text = strerror, per XrdCmsProtocol).
12 * WHY: Plane B write-cluster parity. The headline security property: a hostile
13 * or compromised manager cannot make a node mutate anything outside its
14 * export root — every path is resolved beneath the export rootfd.
15 * HOW: brix_cms_node_plan() is a PURE mapping (opcode+rrdata -> action) so the
16 * arg handling (mode/size parsing, mv needs two paths) is unit-testable;
17 * the executor applies the plan via the src/path/beneath.h confined helpers.
18 */
