1/*
2 * tape_stage_conf.h — per-server tape/stage directive config.
3 *
4 * WHAT: The `brix_frm_*` directive config struct + its init/merge/watermark
5 * helpers. Embedded as `frm` in ngx_stream_brix_srv_conf_t (name kept for
6 * back-compat) and read by the staging callers (prepare/tape_rest/open) for
7 * enable/stage_ttl/async_recall/stage_wait/control_dir.
8 *
9 * WHY: The FRM-dissolution (§13b, phase-64 P6) moved the staging IMPLEMENTATION to
10 * the composable stage request registry (src/fs/xfer/) + the sd_frm backend, so
11 * src/frm/ was deleted. This header is the config surface that survives: the
12 * directive names + parse/merge, lifted out of the deleted src/frm/frm.h so the
13 * many `brix_frm_*` directives keep working unchanged. The legacy
14 * FRM-implementation knobs (stagecmd/copycmd/residency_cmd/watermarks/queue_path)
15 * are accepted for config compatibility but no longer drive a bespoke engine.
16 */
