1/*
2 * kxr_names.h — kXR wire-vocabulary name tables (single source of truth).
3 *
4 * WHAT: Pure int -> const char* lookups for kXR request opcodes, response status
5 * codes, and error codes, keyed on the constants in src/protocol/opcodes.h.
6 * WHY: Both the native client (wire-trace + status reporting) and, in future, the
7 * module's logging want human names for the same wire codes; keeping ONE
8 * table next to opcodes.h prevents the two sides drifting.
9 * HOW: Plain switch statements over the opcodes.h #defines — no allocation, no
10 * ngx, no OpenSSL. Compiles unchanged into libxrdproto (ngx-free core).
11 *
12 * Clean-room: names come from the documented XRootD wire protocol, not XrdCl.
13 */
