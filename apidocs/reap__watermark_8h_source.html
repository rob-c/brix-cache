4/*
5 * reap_watermark.h — proactive, watermark-driven LRU reaper for the read cache.
6 *
7 * WHAT: A background per-worker timer that purges the read cache oldest-first
8 * when filesystem occupancy crosses the HIGH watermark, down to the LOW
9 * watermark (hysteresis), independent of cache fills.
10 *
11 * WHY: The on-fill safety net (brix_cache_evict_if_needed) only runs while
12 * the cache is being written. A cache that fills and then goes quiet would
13 * never shrink. The timer keeps occupancy bounded under any traffic shape,
14 * the way XCache's periodic purge does.
15 *
16 * HOW: brix_cache_watermark_purge() samples occupancy (TTL-cached), and if it
17 * exceeds HIGH takes the cross-worker eviction lock and drives the shared
18 * engine brix_cache_purge_to_target() down to LOW. The timer handler
19 * re-arms itself at cache_reap_interval.
20 */
