1/* ---- File: src/core/negcache/negcache.h — negative-path backoff (ngx side) ----
2 *
3 * E-4 DoS resilience. The ngx-facing half of the negative-path backoff: it owns
4 * the cross-worker SHM slot array + mutex, derives the connection's principal
5 * hash (token subject → GSI DN → client IP, in that order), and drives the
6 * ngx-free core (negcache_core.h). The kXR handlers call note_miss() at a
7 * kXR_NotFound outcome; a non-zero return is the kXR_wait backoff to send in
8 * place of the NotFound.
9 */
