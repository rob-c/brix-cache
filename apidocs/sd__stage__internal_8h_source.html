5 * sd_stage_internal.h — declarations shared between the two halves of the
6 * write-stage decorator after the phase-79 file-size split.

8 * WHAT: Cross-declares the decorator's shared instance state, the driver
9 * descriptor, and every method that crosses the sd_stage.c /
10 * sd_stage_write.c file boundary.
11 * WHY: sd_stage.c (the decorator core: read/namespace/xattr/dir forwarders, the
12 * open dispatch, the driver descriptor, and instance lifecycle) and
13 * sd_stage_write.c (the two interposed WRITE paths — the write-back
14 * byte-I/O object and the staged-upload path) were one 725-line file;

16 * lives in sd_stage.c and dispatches to the write methods in
17 * sd_stage_write.c, while the write-back open in sd_stage_write.c stamps
18 * objects with the driver defined in sd_stage.c — so exactly those symbols
19 * become non-static and are declared here.
20 * HOW: Both translation units include this header; nothing here is exported
