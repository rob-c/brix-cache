2 * vfs_read.c — the shared full-read primitive.
3 *
4 * WHAT: Implements brix_vfs_pread_full(), the EINTR-safe, short-read-tolerant
5 * pread loop used throughout the VFS (the I/O core in vfs_io_core.c and the
6 * per-buffer write copier, plus the AIO offload bodies).
7 *
8 * WHY: Every VFS read has to apply the same short-read/EINTR retry policy, and
9 * every data byte has to traverse the Storage Driver seam rather than a raw
10 * syscall. Concentrating the loop here guarantees both, once.
11 *
12 * HOW: brix_vfs_pread_full() wraps the fd in a POSIX storage-driver object and
13 * drives driver.pread() in a loop until len bytes are read or EOF, retrying
14 * on EINTR and reporting the byte count in *nread even on error.
15 */
