4/*
5 * session.h — SSI session + RRTable.
6 *
7 * WHAT: one open "/.ssi/<service>" handle becomes a session multiplexing many
8 * concurrent requests, each keyed by the reqId carried in the RRInfo.
9 * WHY: real libXrdSsi clients pipeline several requests on one resource handle.
10 * HOW: a fixed-size table of per-reqId request slots; find-or-create on write,
11 * lookup on query/read, drop on cancel/complete. Pool-allocated under the
12 * connection pool so teardown is automatic.
13 *
14 * Standalone unit tests compile this file with -DSSI_UT_STANDALONE and a libc
15 * malloc/free shim instead of the nginx pool.
