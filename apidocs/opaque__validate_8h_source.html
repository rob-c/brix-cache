6/*
7 * opaque_validate.h — ABI for the two-tier XRootD CGI opaque gate
8 * (hyper-hardening-plan §D-2). Pure C, no nginx/libc-string deps: safe to
9 * include from the wire edge and from a standalone unit test alike.
10 *
11 * Tier 1 — byte-hygiene (always on): brix_opaque_illegal_byte() rejects a
12 * control / non-ASCII / shell-metacharacter byte before any handler
13 * parses, logs, or forwards the string. Zero false positives — a
14 * conforming client percent-encodes anything outside the set.
15 *
16 * Tier 2 — schema (opt-in via brix_opaque_strict): brix_opaque_schema_check()
17 * parses the key=value pairs and enforces (a) a positive-integer type
18 * on the keys brix assigns a type to (oss.asize) and (b) a recognized-
19 * namespace vocabulary, rejecting a key in no known namespace. OFF by
20 * default — stock XRootD leaves both unenforced, so enabling it is an
21 * operator's deliberate posture choice and cannot regress parity.
22 */
23
