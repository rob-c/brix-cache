4/*
5 * manager/redir_cache.h — short-lived redirect-collapse cache.
6 *
7 * When brix_collapse_redir is on, successful path→DS redirect results are
8 * stored here so repeat requests for the same path skip the CMS round-trip.
9 * The cache lives in a shared-memory zone so all worker processes share one
10 * consistent view. Entries expire after brix_collapse_redir_ttl ms.
11 *
12 * API:
13 * brix_redir_cache_configure() — call once from postconfiguration.
14 * brix_redir_cache_lookup() — returns 1 and fills host/port on hit.
15 * brix_redir_cache_insert() — stores a resolved redirect with TTL.
16 */
