30/*
31 * brix_gsi_verify_chain — verify an x.509 proxy certificate chain.
32 *
33 * Parameters
34 * log — nginx log; error messages are written here on failure
35 * store — pre-loaded X509_STORE of trusted CA certificates
36 * leaf — end-entity / leaf certificate (not NULL)
37 * untrusted — STACK_OF(X509) of intermediate certs, or NULL if none
38 * verify_depth — maximum proxy chain depth (0 = use OpenSSL default)
39 * res — caller-allocated output; res->dn_buf set on NGX_OK
40 * client_purpose — 1 for the WebDAV/TLS client-cert path: proxy chains are
41 * NOT accepted and the leaf must be usable for client auth
42 * (clientAuth/anyEKU + digitalSignature/keyAgreement). 0 for
43 * the root:// GSI path: RFC 3820 proxy chains are accepted.
44 *
45 * In both modes every cert in the verified chain is held to the per-cert
46 * conformance policy (key strength, no MD5/SHA-1, well-formed serial, no
47 * control bytes in the DN) and any proxyCertInfo must be critical with a
48 * recognised policy OID.
49 *
50 * Returns NGX_OK when the chain is valid; res->dn_buf is then
51 * NUL-terminated and holds the subject DN of the verified leaf.
52 * Returns NGX_ERROR on any failure (already logged); res is zeroed.
53 *
54 * Ownership: the function does NOT take ownership of leaf, untrusted,
55 * or store. All resources remain the caller's responsibility.
56 */
