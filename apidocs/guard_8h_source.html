4/*
5 * guard.h — protocol-agnostic bad-actor classifier (pure C, no nginx).
6 *
7 * WHAT: normalizes any protocol request into guard_request_t, then classifies it
8 * for bad-actor signals (junk signatures, namespace-grammar violations,
9 * backend not-found storms, auth failures) and formats one audit line per
10 * flagged request for fail2ban to ban on.
11 * WHY: the bad-actor logic and the fail2ban contract are identical across ARC,
12 * XrdHttp/WebDAV, and root:// — write them once, feed from thin adapters.
13 * HOW: pure C — no nginx, no allocation, no OpenSSL — so it embeds in an nginx
14 * http module, in the stream relay, and unit-tests standalone. Mirrors
15 * src/net/tap/.
16 */
