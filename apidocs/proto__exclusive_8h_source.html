4/*
5 * proto_exclusive.h - config-load guard "one brix protocol per location, per port".
6 *
7 * WHAT: A single config-time check, brix_http_proto_exclusive_check(), that
8 * rejects a configuration enabling more than one brix HTTP protocol
9 * (brix_webdav, brix_s3, brix_cvmfs) in the same location, or mixing two
10 * different brix protocols under one listen port.
11 *
12 * WHY: The unified per-location storage directives (brix_export,
13 * brix_cache_store, ...) belong to exactly one owning protocol; allowing
14 * two protocols to claim the same location makes their meaning
15 * ambiguous. One-protocol-per-port is the intended deployment model, so a
16 * mismatch is a configuration error caught at load time, not at runtime.
17 *
18 * HOW: Called at the end of the WebDAV postconfiguration (which nginx runs
19 * after ALL module merges, so every enable flag is final). Returns NGX_OK
20 * when the configuration is consistent, or logs an EMERG diagnostic and
21 * returns NGX_ERROR to fail `nginx -t`.
22 */
