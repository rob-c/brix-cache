7/*
8 * ============================================================
9 * WHAT: SigV4 credential components and parser declarations.
10 * ============================================================
11 *
12 * This header defines the parsed representation of AWS Signature V4
13 * credentials (access key, date, region, signed headers, signature)
14 * and declares the two parsers that populate it:
15 * - parse_authorization() — parses the Authorization header
16 * from a signed request.
17 * - parse_presigned_authorization() — parses X-Amz-* query params
18 * from a presigned URL.
19 *
20 * The sigv4_components_t struct is shared across auth_sigv4_parse.c,
21 * auth_sigv4_canonical.c, and auth_sigv4_verify.c so the canonical
22 * string builder and signature verifier operate on identical parsed data.
23 * ============================================================
24 */
