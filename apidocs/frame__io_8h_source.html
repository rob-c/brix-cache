6/* ---- CMS frame send helpers — shared wire transmission routines ----
7 *
8 * WHAT: Interface for sending CMS manager protocol frames over a persistent
9 * TCP connection. Two primitives: send_all (looping buffer transmit)
10 * and send_frame (header construction + payload dispatch).
11 *
12 * WHY: The CMS heartbeat protocol requires every outgoing message to carry an
13 * 8-byte big-endian header followed by variable-length payload. Callers
14 * in send.c build payloads but delegate transmission to these helpers,
15 * avoiding duplicated send-loop logic across login/load/avail/pong frames.
16 *
17 * HOW: brix_cms_send_all() loops c->send() until len bytes are transmitted;
18 * returns NGX_AGAIN on partial write (caller retries), NGX_ERROR on failure,
19 * NGX_OK on full success. brix_cms_send_frame() constructs the header
20 * via ngx_brix_cms_put32/put16 from cms_internal.h then dispatches both
21 * header and payload through send_all(). */
