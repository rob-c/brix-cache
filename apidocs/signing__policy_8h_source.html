1/*
2 * signing_policy.h — Globus EACL signing_policy parser + subject matcher.
3 *
4 * WHAT: Parses a single <hash>.signing_policy file image into a compiled
5 * policy (per-CA cond_subjects glob lists) and answers "may this CA
6 * sign this subject DN?".
7 * WHY: WLCG/IGTF trust requires a CA sign only within its namespace; plain
8 * PKIX chain validation does not enforce this. A trusted-but-misbehaving
9 * CA that signs outside its delegated namespace is accepted by OpenSSL
10 * but MUST be rejected under the WLCG trust model.
11 * HOW: ngx-free, caller-owned allocation, no globals. Fail closed on any
12 * malformed input (the caller rejects the CA). DN comparison is on the
13 * OpenSSL oneline slash form, case-insensitive.
14 */
