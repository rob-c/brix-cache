1/*
2 * sss_keytab.h — SSS (Simple Shared Secret) keytab I/O + credential crypto.
3 *
4 * WHAT: Read/write the text SSS keytab, plus the two crypto primitives an SSS
5 * credential needs: the IEEE CRC32 integrity word and Blowfish-CFB64
6 * encryption. Shared by sec_sss.c (the auth module) and xrdsssadmin.c.
7 * WHY: The native client must speak SSS to an XRootD server byte-for-byte. The
8 * server's own implementation (src/auth/sss/) is nginx-coupled, so the small,
9 * standard kernels are reimplemented here from the wire spec (NOT copied
10 * from XrdSecsss) — clean-room. The CRC is IEEE (poly 0xedb88320), NOT the
11 * Castagnoli crc32c in libxrdproto.
12 * HOW: Keytab line: "0 N:<id> k:<hexkey> u:<user> g:<group> n:<name> [e:<exp>]"
13 * (mode 0600, O_NOFOLLOW). bf32_encrypt uses EVP_bf_cfb64, padding off, a
14 * variable key length, an all-zero 8-byte IV, and loads the OpenSSL-3
15 * "legacy" provider once.
16 *
17 * Cross-checked against src/auth/sss/{config.c,auth_crypto_helpers.c,
18 * auth_proxy_credential.c}; see docs/refactor/phase-37-clean-room-log.md.
19 */
